How it works:

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method where you divide your tasks
into 25 minute focused blocks of time.

Start the
Pomodoro timer

Work until the
timer rings

Take a short break
3-5 mins

Repeat
steps 1-3

Rules & Tips

Work on your task until the Pomodoro timer rings.

Breaks are mandatory. You need this time for your mind to refresh before the next task.

After completing four Pomodoros, take a longer break - 15 to 30 minutes.

The Pomodoro Technique
